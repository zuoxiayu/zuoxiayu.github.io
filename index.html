I am currently a postdoctoral fellow at the Department of Computer Science of the Univerisity of Hong Kong. Before that, I earned my PhD degree in Feb 2020 from the Hong Kong Polytechnic University, where I was advised by Prof. Man Ho Au. I received my B.E. in 2012 and MPhil in 2015 from Shandong University. I will join the University of Wollongong as a lecturer (~US Assistant Professor) in Cyber Security at School of Computing & Information Technology in 2021.

I am broadly interested in applied cryptography, including:

  • Privacy-Preserving Cryptography
  • Blockchain Technology & Cryptocurrency
  • Lattice-based Cryptography
